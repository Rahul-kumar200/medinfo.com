About Us

WELCOME to MEDINFO...

We are here to help you and we also need your help.
Medinfo is a non-profit organisation which is made with the purpose of charity.
Medinfo is basically a where you will get to know about the availability of medicines, beds, oxygen-cylinder, vaccines and many more things which are difficult to arrange for a middle class person.
We use some forms to deal with it.
If anyone is in urgent need of medicines/ injections etc., we check the availability, and accordingly provide it to the needy ones.
One section is for donation of such things where if any person has the unused medicines etc. and wish to donate them, so he can fill the donation form and and provide the help.
We hope that our website will help you in providing medicines and other Covid related facilities.
For more details you can contact us on various social media platforms.
